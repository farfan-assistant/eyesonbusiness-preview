FAQ

Straight answers before someone buys a spot.

These are the most common questions business owners ask before reserving space on a shared postcard.

What am I actually buying?OpenClose

A reserved spot on a shared postcard mailed to a selected residential route, plus help with the offer, the ad, and mailing coordination.

Do I share the card with competitors?OpenClose

No. The model is one business per category per card. If your category is already taken, it is closed for that drop.

How do you choose the neighborhoods?OpenClose

Routes are picked first, based on the kind of business being promoted. The goal is to match the homes to the offer, not stuff random advertisers onto a card and hope for the best.

What kinds of businesses tend to do best?OpenClose

Home services, dental, wellness, restaurants, salons, and other local businesses with a clear homeowner offer usually fit best.

Do you handle the ad design too?OpenClose

Yes. We help put together a clean ad with one clear offer, sensible copy, and the details a homeowner needs to act.

When do you collect payment?OpenClose

Before the spot is reserved and before design starts. That keeps the campaign real and avoids soft holds that waste time.

Is this meant to replace Facebook or Google ads?OpenClose

No. This works better as an additional local channel. It gives you physical visibility in the mailbox while your digital ads do their own job.

How do we know if it worked?OpenClose

The first signals are simple: scans, calls, messages, and whether the business wants another drop in the same or a better route.
