FAQ

Questions business owners usually ask before reserving a spot.

If you are comparing this to other local marketing channels, these are the details that matter most up front.

What am I actually buying?OpenClose

A reserved spot on a shared postcard mailed to a selected residential route, along with ad design support and mailing coordination.

Do I share the card with competitors?OpenClose

No. The postcard is sold one business per category, so a direct competitor is not placed beside you on the same mailing.

How do you choose the neighborhoods?OpenClose

Routes are selected based on the type of customer the business wants to reach. The goal is to match the homes to the offer.

Which businesses usually fit best?OpenClose

Home services, dental, wellness, restaurants, salons, and other local businesses with a clear neighborhood offer tend to fit best.

Do you handle the ad design too?OpenClose

Yes. The spot includes help with the ad layout, the headline, and the main offer so the postcard is easier for homeowners to respond to.

When is payment collected?OpenClose

Before the spot is reserved and before design begins. That keeps the schedule clean and avoids holding space without commitment.

Is this meant to replace digital ads?OpenClose

No. It works well as an additional local channel, especially for businesses that want physical visibility in the mailbox alongside their digital marketing.

How can response be tracked?OpenClose

The strongest version includes a clear offer and a trackable action such as a QR code, phone number, or landing page tied to the campaign.
